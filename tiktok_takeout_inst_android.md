# Android Instructions
<div style="padding-top:20px;"></div>
## Step 1: Access Your Account and Request Your Data

<img src="images/Android/android_1.png" width="250" style="margin:auto;">
1. In your Tik Tok app, tap the ☰ in the upper right corner. Then tap "Settings and privacy".
<img src="images/Android/android_2.png" width="250" style="margin:auto;">
2. Tap "Account".
<img src="images/Android/android_3.png" width="250" style="margin:auto;">
3. Tap "Download your data".
<img src="images/Android/android_4.png" width="250" style="margin:auto;">
4. Select a "JSON" file format and tap "Done".
<img src="images/Android/android_5.png" width="250" style="margin:auto;">
5. Tap "Request data".
<div style="padding-top:30px;"></div>

<h1 class="blink-two" ><center><strong style="font-size: 30px; color: #FF0000">STOP!</strong></center></h1> 
			<p>Please allwo up to <strong>1-4</strong> days for your data to be prepared before proceeding to the next steps.</p>
<br/>

*Note: if your file size is larger than 1 GB, you most likely selected more data than requested. Please make sure you unselect “videos” in step 4.*

## Step 2: Download Your Data

<img src="images/Android/android_6.png" width="250" style="margin:auto;">
6. Return to your "Download Tik Tok data" page ((Menu > Settings and privacy > Account > Download Your Data)
<img src="images/Android/android_7.png" width="250" style="margin:auto;">
7. At the top, there are two tabs. Tap the second, "Download data". Then select "Download".
<img src="images/Android/android_8.png" width="250" style="margin:auto;">
<img src="images/Android/android_9.png" width="250" style="margin:auto;">
8. If prompted, authenticate your account with your gmail or phone number.
<img src="images/Android/android_10.png" width="250" style="margin:auto;">
9. This will bring up a page in your browser. Once the page has loaded, press "Open file" at the bottom of the screen.
<div style="padding-top:30px;"></div>

## Step 3: Share Your Data with Researchers

<img src="images/Android/android_11.png" width="250" style="margin:auto;">
10. Navigate to the data upload page located at: <a href="https://nyu.app.box.com/f/c7acc7cbc97c4b76a6589394b53b235f">https://nyu.app.box.com/f/c7acc7cbc97c4b76a6589394b53b235f</a> and click "Select Files".
<img src="images/Android/android_12.png" width="250" style="margin:auto;">
11. This will take you to your phone’s files. Select "Downloads".
On other Android devices, you may need to navigate to the “downloads” folder, such as "media picker".
<img src="images/Android/android_13.png" width="250" style="margin:auto;">
12. Once within the Downloads file, Select the TikTok file and press "Extract" at the bottom of the screen. Other Android devices might take you directly to the upload.
<img src="images/Android/android_14.png" width="250" style="margin:auto;">
12. Once you see your file in "Uploaded files" back on the Box upload page, press "Submit" at the bottom of the screen.

<img src="images/Android/android_15.png" width="250" style="margin:auto;">
13. You will know your file is uploaded once you see this screen. You can then exit your browser.
